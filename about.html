WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT US
Welcome to MYHEALTH DR.

A comprehensive health mobile app that enables users to consult with certified doctors across various specialities, book appointments effortlessly, and securely diagnoses and medical records in a centralized database. The app will also feature a geolocation tool to help users quickly locate and navigate to nearby hosipitals, ensuring timely access to healthcare services. By combining telemedicine, digital health management, and location -based services, the app bridges gaps in Nigeria's healthcare system, offering a user-friendly, secure, and eficient solution for patients.






What We Do

A mobile app with functionalities for consulting doctors, booking appointments, storing diagnoses, and locating nearby hosipitals in Nigeria which would offer significant benefits in a region where healthcare access can be limited.
